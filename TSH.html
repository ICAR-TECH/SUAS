TSH: Three-Year Seed-Utilization Heatmap

About the Tool

This tool simulates crop area expansion driven by seed production and utilization efficiency over three successive years. The resulting heatmap quantifies the percentage of total area covered after Year-3 under different seed utilization scenarios. It supports CFLD impact assessment, seed system planning, varietal dissemination strategies, and long-term agricultural scaling decisions. The visualization helps identify saturation thresholds, efficiency gaps, and optimal utilization pathways for policymakers and researchers.
